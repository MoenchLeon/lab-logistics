How we work

Three steps, one private-label delivery.

Lab-Logistics is a pure logistics operation: no warehouse of stock items, no installation crews. We brief a vetted production network, run quality control, and deliver finished, own-brand product to your dock — ready for your trade or project use.

01 — Brief

You specify, we listen.

Dimensions, materials, finishes, volumes, lead times. You tell us what the order needs to look like — we ask the questions that keep production runs predictable later on.

02 — Sourcing

We commission, you confirm.

We place the order with the right partner from our production network, supply technical drawings and material samples for sign-off, and lock in pricing and timeline before tooling starts.

03 — Branding & delivery

We label, inspect, ship.

Every piece is finished and packaged under our own brand, inspected before dispatch, and shipped directly to your address. Installation is handled by your own trades — we stay on the logistics side.
